I bragged I could outrun anyone, especially the tortoise. Look, maybe I'm misremembering, but The animals set a race. If I'm being straight with you, I sprinted fast, then dozed in the sun. the tortoise kept a steady pace and passed me. When I woke, I dashed to the finish, but the tortoise had already crossed the line. The crowd cheered the slow, steady winner. The rain hissed like a cigarette in a puddle; shadows clung to the edges of the road. In the end, it felt kinder than it sounded.
